# Instagram Brand Launch Plan: Creator Armour 🚀

This plan outlines the strategy and assets for launching Creator Armour on Instagram. We've designed a 9-grid launch sequence that tells a cohesive story of trust, power, and protection.

## The Aesthetic: Premium Glassmorphism
The visual identity for this launch is built on **Dark Premium Glassmorphism**. We use deep blacks, vibrant emerald greens, and sky blue gradients with translucent UI elements to create a futuristic, "safe-yet-powerful" feel.

## Execution Roadmap
1. **Tease (Day 1-2)**: Use Stories with Post 1 (blurred) and countdown stickers.
2. **The Reveal (Day 3)**: Drop the first 3 posts simultaneously to establish the grid theme.
3. **Deep Dive (Day 4-8)**: Drop one post per day, highlighting a specific feature (Escrow, Legal, Analytics).
4. **The Big Push (Day 9)**: Final post with a clear CTA to Sign Up/Join Waitlist.
