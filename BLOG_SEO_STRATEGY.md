## 🔥 Content Optimization Checklist

For each blog post:

- [ ] **Title:** 55-60 characters, includes primary keyword
- [ ] **Meta Description:** 150-160 characters, includes keyword
- [ ] **H1:** Unique per page, includes keyword
- [ ] **H2s:** 3-5 headings with secondary keywords
- [ ] **Word Count:** 1500-2000 words (optimal for SEO)
- [ ] **Internal Links:** 2-3 links to relevant pages
- [ ] **Images:** 1-2 images with alt text
- [ ] **Schema Markup:** Article schema added
- [ ] **FAQ:** Add FAQ section with FAQ schema
